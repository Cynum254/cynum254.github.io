About me

I came upon a survey that showed the underrepresentation of women in the tech field, particularly women of color. My curiosity was peaked and I decided to do some research on web development.

I decided to take an online course to receive the appropriate training and certification required to embark on this newfound hobby. I started making websites for friends and family to not only create a strong portfolio, but to encourage others to do the same. I am proficient in html 5, CSS 3, Javascript and React.

Today, I receive countless emails from African women across the continent enquiring for the blueprint of my success in the world of web development.
